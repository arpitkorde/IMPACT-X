# Governance

IMPACT-X is currently maintained by its founder with a clear, written path to community governance.

---

## Current Phase: Founder Governance (v0.x)

During the initial phase, the founder makes all decisions about framework direction, contribution acceptance, and version releases. This is not because community input isn't valued — it is because good governance requires enough shared context for decisions to be made well, and that context takes time to build.

**What this means in practice:**
- All pull requests are reviewed and merged by the founder
- Framework challenges are evaluated by the founder with community input
- Version releases are decided by the founder

---

## Path to Community Governance

When IMPACT-X reaches the following milestones, governance moves to a Community Committee:

- **50 contributors** across at least 5 different industries
- **10 documented case studies** with real pilot data
- **3 active vertical adaptations** maintained by community members

At that point, a Community Committee of 5 members will be elected — one from each of the five most active vertical communities. The Committee will govern by majority vote, with the founder holding one seat permanently as a non-voting tie-breaker.

---

## Decision Types

**Community decisions** (open issues, 7-day discussion period):
- New dimensions or changes to existing dimensions
- Changes to the scoring formulas
- Addition of new official verticals
- Major version releases

**Maintainer decisions** (no discussion period required):
- Accepting or rejecting pull requests for documentation and templates
- Accepting or rejecting agent registry entries
- Minor version releases and bug fixes
- Moderation decisions under the Code of Conduct

---

## Versioning

IMPACT-X follows semantic versioning:

- **Major versions** (1.0, 2.0): Significant changes to the framework's core dimensions or philosophy. Require community decision.
- **Minor versions** (0.1, 0.2): New templates, verticals, or scoring refinements. Maintainer decision with community input.
- **Patch versions** (0.1.1): Corrections, clarifications, typos. Maintainer decision.

---

*Governance model subject to revision as the community grows. Changes to this document require a community decision.*
